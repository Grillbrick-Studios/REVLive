Instructions for installing the REV into “e-Sword X” on a Mac

  1. You must have e-Sword X installed on your Mac. The program is available for $10.00 in the App Store.
  2. Make sure that e-Sword X app is not running.
  3. In Safari, download the REV module you want from the Export section of the REV website.
  4. Once the download is complete, open Finder and click on the "Downloads" folder. All you have to do is double-click on the file you just downloaded and e-Sword X will open and load the file and the REV will be available from within the program.
