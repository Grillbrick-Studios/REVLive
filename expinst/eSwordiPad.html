Instructions for installing the REV into “e-Sword HD” on an iPad

  1. You must have e-Sword HD installed on your iPad. The app is available for the nominal cost of $5.00 in the App Store.
  2. Make sure that e-Sword HD app is not running. You can do this by double-clicking the Home button on your iPad and swipe all running apps off the top of the screen.
  3. In Safari, go to the Export section of the REV website and choose the module you want to download. Make sure the "e-Sword" program is selected.
  4. Click "Click here" to download the file:
  5. When the file has downloaded, you will see this screen:
  6. You may see a link that says "Open with...", or "More...". Tap on that link, and you will see this:
  7. Tap on the "Copy to e-Sword HD" icon and the e-Sword HD app will open and the REV Bible will be available within the app.
