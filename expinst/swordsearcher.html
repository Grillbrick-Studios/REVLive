Instructions for installing the REV into “Swordsearcher” on a Windows PC

  1. You must have Swordsearcher installed on your PC. A trial version is available for free at https://www.swordsearcher.com/download.html. Or you may purchase the full version at that same link for $59.95.
  2. Make sure that the Swordsearcher program is not running.
  3. Download the Swordsearcher file from the Export section of the REV website, keeping track of where your browser is saving the file to. Usually, this will be in your “Downloads” folder, as seen here in Windows Explorer. There are three files in the zip file.
  4. Copy or move the files to the Swordsearcher modules folder. Usually, this is “C:\Program Files\Swordsearcher\modules”.
  5. Start Swordsearcher, and the REV Bible, Commentary, and Appendices will be available within the program.
