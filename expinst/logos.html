Instructions for installing the REV into “Logos”

Installing the REV Bible

  1. You must have Logos installed on your Computer. The program is available in both free and paid versions. The free version is available here.
  2. Download the REV_Logos.zip file and extract it to a place on your computer that you can locate.
  3. Start the Logos program, and on the left side click "Tools," then scroll down to "Utilities," and select "Personal Books."
  4. In the panel that opens up, click "add book."
  5. Fill in the fields as shown. Make sure that for "Type" you choose "Bible." For the logo, click the "Change..." link and select the "REV_Icon.png" file from the place you extracted the "REV_Logos.zip" file. Click "Add file..." and select the "REV_Logos_Bible.docx" file from the same location. Once the fields are filled in, click the "Build book" button in the lower right corner.
  6. Logos will process the file, and the REV Bible will now be available within Logos.

Installing the REV Commentary

  1. The process for installing the REV Commentary is exactly the same, except make sure that for "Type" you choose "Bible Commentary," and select the "REV_Logos_Commentary" file.
