Instructions for installing the REV into “e-Sword” on a Windows PC

  1. You must have e-Sword installed on your PC. The program is available for free at https://www.e-sword.net/.
  2. Make sure that the e-Sword program is not running.
  3. Download the REV module(s) you want to install from the Export section of the REV website, keeping track of where your browser is saving the files to. Usually, this will be in your “Downloads” folder, as seen here in Windows Explorer.
  4. Copy or move the files to the e-Sword installation folder. Usually, this is “C:\Program Files (x86)\e-Sword”.
  5. Start e-Sword, and the REV files you downloaded should be available within the program.
