Instructions for installing the REV into “theWord” on a Windows PC

  1. You must have theWord installed on your PC. The program is available for free at http://www.theword.net/.
  2. Make sure that theWord is not running.
  3. Download the REV Bible module from the Export section of the REV website, keeping track of where your browser is saving the file to. Usually, this will be in your “Downloads” folder, as seen here in Windows Explorer.
  4. Double-click the file and theWord will open and offer to install the REV Bible.
  5. Go ahead and click “Install selected modules” and the REV Bible be available within the program.
