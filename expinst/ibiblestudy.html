Instructions for installing the REV into “iBible-Study HD” on an iPad

Apologies, these instructions are out of date. They will be updated soon.

  1. You must have iBible-Study HD installed on your iPad. The app is available for free in the App Store.
  2. Make sure that iBible-Study HD app is not running. You can do this by double-clicking the Home button on your iPad and swipe all running apps off the top of the screen.
  3. In Safari, go to the Export section of the REV website and choose the module you want to download. Make sure the "iBible-Study HD" app is selected, and then click "Proceed".
  4. Click "Click here" to download the file:
  5. When the file has downloaded, you will see this screen:
  6. If you see "Open in iBible-Study HD", go ahead and tap it, and you are done. If you do NOT see "Open in iBible-Study HD", click "More...", and you will see this:
  7. Tap on the "Copy to iBible-Study HD" icon and the iBible-Study HD app will open and the REV Bible will be available within the app.
