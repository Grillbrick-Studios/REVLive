Instructions for installing the REV into “e-Sword LT” on an iPhone

  1. You must have e-Sword LT installed on your iPhone. The app is available for the nominal cost of $3.00 in the App Store.
  2. Make sure that e-Sword LT app is not running. You can do this by double-clicking the Home button on your phone and swipe all running apps off the top of the screen.
  3. In Safari, download the REV module you want from the Export section of the REV website.
  4. Once the download is complete, you will see this screen:
  5. You may see a link that says "Open with...", or "More...". Tap on that link, and you will see this:
  6. Tap on the "Copy to e-Sword LT" icon and the e-Sword LT app will open and the REV Bible be available within the app.
